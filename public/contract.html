MUSHADELIC RECORDS AGREEMENT between MUSHADELIC RECORDS and the

Hereinafter DJ/PRODUCER/DJ PRODUCER/SINGER/OTHER will be referred to as Member.
1. Exclusivity.
Parties agree on the agreement duration of 5 years, from the signature date. Label agrees
that this agreement is exclusive worldwide. Client shall not sell or attempt to sell any Label
Content without proper written approval from Mushadelic Records.
Label may not directly contact, solicit and/or engage in business directly with members
during the Term of this Agreement. The Members may not redistribute content hereby
agreed to be distributed by Mushadelic Records through the entire duration of this
contract. Members may not sign any contracts or agreements involving content
distributed by Mushadelic Records without Labels consent.
2. Intellectual Property Ownership.
The Label warrants and represents that it has all rights of Members Content provided to
Mushadelic Records (including but not limited to masters, videos, sound recordings, artist
names, song names, artwork and images) and/or has all rights necessary to grant the
licenses granted herein. Label also guarantees that it has secured written permission or
waivers of rights with any necessary persons or third parties that have granted Label
permission to distribute the content through Mushadelic Records. Label hereby agrees to not
deliver any Members Content that is not legally owned or secured via
permission. If any material contains Content of any kind that is not cleared, licensed, and/or
owned by the Members, Mushadelic Records is exempt from all legal issues and/or
wrongdoing, and Members agrees to indemnify to Mushadelic Records for the losses to be
accounted.
3. Members
Mushadelic Records agrees to distribute and license Member's recordings to Partners that sell,
distribute, transmit, stream, perform or otherwise exploit sound and/or audiovisual
recordings by all means and media available, and to collect all income deriving there from.
Mushadelic Record shall (Through our technology partner) solicit and service the Members
secure the encoding of each Recording in format(s) required by the Partners, process the
delivery of the recordings to the Partners, and collect amounts due from the Partners for
repayment to Label, subject to the client account revenue threshold requirements outlined
in this Agreement.
4. Communication with Members.
It is stipulated that communication about this contract and its terms will be unique and
exclusive between Mushadelic Records and the Member signing this agreement will always be
contacted through email if the subject is related to this contract and its terms, in order to
centralize all information for both parts.
5. Territory.
The parts agree that this contract shall be valid worldwide. Meaning that content released by
Mushadelic Records will be protected worldwide from any legal trespassing on these terms hereby
agreed and/or other violations of copyright.
6. Inappropriate Content.
Mushadelic Records will not distribute scandalous, illegal, hateful, highly objectionable or offensive
material. Mushadelic Records and Partners reserve the right to reject distribution of any Member
Content at its discretion.
1. Subject of the Agreement
a. The Label and Member agree to enter into this agreement with the goal of
exclusively selling and distributing the Members products via Mushadelic Records.
b. Its agreed between the parties that for the entire duration of the agreement all stores
supplied by Mushadelic Records on behalf of the Member will be delivered exclusively by
Mushadelic Records, in the event Mushadelic Records aggregates a new 3rd party vendor the
Member can decide if they wish to opt-in or opt-out.
c. Its agreed between the parties that for the entire duration of the agreement, Members
uploaded products will be held and distributed by Mushadelic Records, the Member has the
right to request for product removal from Mushadelic Records and digital distributors, with
the removal fee of $USD100 by product to pay for distributor removal services. If the
agreement ends after its duration without parties renewal, the product will be forever held by
Mushadelic Records and its distributors.
2. Grants of Rights
a. The Member hereby grants Mushadelic Records in the territory, during the term the
rights to convert, bundle, sell, use, copy, distribute, produce, issue copies to the public,
perform, cut, broadcast, communicate, make available to the public and exploit the
products in relation to Mushadelic Records service.
b. To use the Members name and trademarks, as well as the Members artist information
and likeness, for the purposes of promoting the product.
c. To authorize or license any 3rd party to do any of the above.
3. Member Commitments and Responsibilities
a. Mushadelic Records agrees to be responsible for any and all clearances and/or payments in
respect of the products and/or granted rights.
b. Mushadelic Records agrees to be responsible for any/or all clearances or payments in
respect of
the publishing rights for sales made in the territory.
c. Mushadelic Records agrees that the Member will provide pre-listening files in low
resolution format and all information contained in the METADATA.
d. The Member under this agreement, excludes any right to Mushadelic Records to do
remixes, edits, alterations of the master tracks, graphic files, video clips or any other
copyright protected material, unless previously agreed and authorized by Member.
4. Mushadelic Records Commitments and Responsibilities
a. Mushadelic Records commits to convert the content into such format or formats
necessary or desirable to create or otherwise develop and provide the service.
b. Mushadelic Records grants to the Member that all label royalty payments are net
payments to the Member. Mushadelic Records shall not deduct any fees for technology,
transactions, mechanical payments or any other processes involved.
5. Royalty and Accounting
a. Every quarter Mushadelic Records shall account the royalties due and shall provide to
Member with a reasonable detailed calculation of the royalties in respect of activity in the
proceeding quarter(s). Also Mushadelic Records can account the royalties owed quarterly.
b. If the Member's royalties exceeds $USD 125 per year Mushadelic Records will pay 70% of
the royalties to the Member. Payments are done quarterly, so if in any quarter, Member has
no yet exceeded $USD 125 in royalties, payment will be delayed to the next quarter.
c. Any Members royalties that fail to meet the minimum of $USD 125 dollars at the end of a
year will be held by Mushadelic Records.
6. Terms Of The Agreement
a. This agreement has an initial length of 5 Year (Five) from the date that we receive your
signed agreement, renewed automatically for another year if not terminated 3 months
(three) prior its end by either party in written. Once terminated and expired, Mushadelic
Records shall destroy all master copies provided by the Member therefore making them
inaccessible.
b. If for any reason either party materially breaches this agreement, the aggrieved party
shall notify the defaulting party in written form. The defaulting party is given 30 days from
the receipt of the notice to fix the material breach. If the defaulting party fails to fix the
breach within 30 days from the receipt of the notice, then the aggrieved party has the right
to terminate this agreement immediately and in its sole option.
c. Mushadelic Records reserves the right to cancel this agreement if The Member drops below
the threshold stated in paragraph b. under Royalty and Accounting section (5), this decision
will be made on a case by case basis.
7. Representations and Warranties
Both parties herewith warrant and represent that they possess the full right and authority
to enter and perform this agreement.
8. Miscellaneous
a. Both parties agree to hold each other harmless from any loss and damage arising from
any claim brought by any 3rd party against the parties to respect to Mushadelic Records
business to 3rd party platforms or partners.
b. Besides this agreement no other issues are agreed upon by between the parties. Any
modification or amendments of this agreement needs to be agreed upon in written form to
become binding to the parties.
c. The parties agree and acknowledge that the relationship of the parties is in the nature of
independent contractors. This agreement will not be deemed to create a partnership or
joint venture, and neither party is the others agent, partner, employee or representative.
